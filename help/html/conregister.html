Register a Player

Registering a player to play on the S3D Connector ladder is a two-step process. First, when you click on the Register button in the Add a player screen, Sea3D checks the site to make sure that your name is unique. If it already exists on the site, you will have to select another one.

Once you have gotten a unique name, Sea3D requires that you enter a password and confirm it to make sure that your player is secure. Sea3D will not allow you to enter a blank password. It is important to remember this password because once you have completed 10 games on the ladder, your login name and password will be automatically registered on the forums and you will be able to post bug reports, feature requests, and join in the online community.
