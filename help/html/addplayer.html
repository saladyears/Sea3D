Add a player

This window allows you to create or edit a player in the player database.

If you are planning on playing only hotseat or tracking games with this player, you only need to fill out the player name. The preferred colors and languages options are only useful in network games.

If you are planning on playing network games with this player, you may select your first and second choice colors for games as well as specify any languages that you speak to help other network players determine if they can communicate with you.

You may also register your player to play on the ladder at the S3D Connector site, or, if you have already created a ladder player on another computer, you may download that player from the site to this computer.

If you want to register a new player to play on the ladder, click the I want to register... checkbox and then click the Register button to bring you to the Register a player window. You must have at least filled out a name or handle before Sea3D will allow you to continue.

If you want to download an existing player from the site to this computer, click on the Download button to bring up the Download a player window.
